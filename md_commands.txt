	# Heading 1

Italics:
	put *on both sides of text*
    or  put _on both sides of text_

Strong:
	put double asterisks ** (same as italics)
	put double underscores __

Strikethrough:
	~~This text~~ is strikethrough

Horizontal Rule:
	--- (triple)
	or 
	___ (triple)

Escape Characters:
	to show * - do \*
	
Blockquote:
	> This is a quote.

	[Link text](link "Title text" (y'know hover))
	Ex: [My Github Profile](https://www.github.com/captain-i3ye "captain-i3ye")

	1. Item 1
	1. Item 2
	1. Item 3

	`<p>This is a paragraph.</p>`
